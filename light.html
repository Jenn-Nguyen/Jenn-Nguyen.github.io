Light Photography

 

Why I Took The Photos

This photo is an outcome of a photoshoot I did, inspired by Brandon Wolfel. Brandon Wolfel is well known for his light photography. He utilises any light source that he has on hand:

  • Natural light
  • Fairy lights
  • Lights from reflections

I wanted to experiment with these light sources to create an aesthetic look.

 

 

How I Did It

In a dim room with LED lights, the coloured lights softly enveloped the area with a cool tone. Using the manual setting on my DSLR camera:

  • I lowered the ISO to 100: This allowed the camera to capture less light dimming the overall picture. However light was still captured due to the aperture and exposure time/shutter speed.
  • Aperture was set to f/4: letting light into the camera.
  • The shutter speed was set to 0.5 seconds.

Though next time I would try to lower the value. That way the camera can capture a freeze shot, making the image sharper and crisper, eliminating any sorts of movement. ISO would need to go up a bit to allow the camera to capture the light
