Who am I?

A teenage girl located in Melbourne. Currently a VCE student (as of 2020) studying Media. I had discovered my love for the subject during a school project, putting together a Year 12 graduation video. Through taking the class over and over again as an elective I found my passion for photography. Ever since then it has been my hobby.

I want to share my love for photography, displaying all the photographs I’ve taken. I want to share the stories behind the photos. How they're captured. Where they're taken. When.

The photos I’ve taken are when I am on adventures. Some are in the moment shots, from photoshoots; they are all memories I have captured over the years.
